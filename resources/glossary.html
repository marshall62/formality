Evenly Divides close

Evenly divides means a number can be divided into equal-sized groups without a remainder left over.
For example, 2 evenly divides 8 because 8 ÷ 2 = 4, but 2 does not evenly divide 9 because 9 ÷ 2 = 4 with a remainder of 1

Percent or Percentage close
Percent measures all or part of a whole. 100 percent is all of something.
Less than 100 percent is part of something:
  •   75 percent is three-quarters of a whole
  •   50 percent is half of a whole
  •   25 percent is one-quarter of a whole
  •   10 percent is one tenth of a whole
  •   50 cents is half of a dollar
  •   5 is half of ten

01 means one, 10 means ten, and 100 means one hundred.
