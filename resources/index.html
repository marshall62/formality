What is 4mality?

4Mality is an Intelligent Tutoring System for 3rd and 4th grade students learning to solve math word problems. It provides practice and preparation for the MCAS and other math achievement tests.

Why use 4mality?

4mality has several characteristics that make it a powerful resource for children, teachers, and families:

  • It teaches the subject matter content and problem solving skills set forth in the Massachusetts Curriculum Framework as well as the National Common Core State Standards.

  • It features four coaches (Estella Explainer, Chef Math Bear, How-to Hound, and Visual Vicuna) who give language, computation, strategy, and visual hints for solving problems.

  • It adapts to the level of knowledge and learning style of every student by using an Artificial Intelligence decision system.

  • It is designed with standarized testing in mind, so that every practice question follows a multiple choice and open response style.

  • It familiarizes the students to high stakes testing by providing continuous feedback in terms of testing scores.

Meet the Coaches!

Estella Explainer rewords the problem to help students gain familiarity with the wording of the MCAS. Estella asks, "What is this question asking?"

Chef Math Bear provides students with one potential strategy to solve the problem. Bear asks, "How can numbers be used to solve the problem?"

How-to Hound looks at the problem from a different angle. Hound asks, "What is another way to solve this problem?"

Visual Vicuna provides graphs and animations to help students understand the question. Vicuna asks, "How does the problem look visually?"
