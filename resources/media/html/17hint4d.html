Look first at what happens when there are just two categories of
combinations:  Paint colors and Carpet colors.
 
3 paint colors combine with navy carpet to make 3 combinations:
 
Paint      Carpet
Blue       Navy
Yellow    Navy       3 paints  x
White      Navy        1 carpet =
                                3 combinations
 
3 paint colors combine with brown carpet to make 3 combinations:
 
Paint      Carpet
Blue       Brown
Yellow    Brown     3 paints  x
White      Brown      1 carpet =
                               3 combinations
 
There are six combinations of paint colors and carpet colors. 3 combinations
+ 3 combinations = 6 or 3 paints x 2 carpets = 3 x 2 = 6  Now combine
wallpaper patterns.  The six Paint and Carpet possibilities combine with
solid wallpaper to make 6 combinations:
 
Paint  Carpet   Wallpaper
Blue     Navy,    Solid
Yellow Navy    Solid       6 paint/
White  Navy   Solid          carpet
Blue, Brown   Solid      combinations
Yellow Brown Solid   x 1 wallpaper
White Brown   Solid       6 paint, carpet
                                      Wallpaper
                                       combinations
 
Paint and Carpet possibilities combine with striped wallpaper to make 6
combinations:
 
Paint  Carpet   Wallpaper
Blue  Navy   Stripe
Yellow Navy  Stripe      6 paint/
White Navy  Stripe       carpet
Blue Brown Stripe       combinations
Yellow Brown Stripe   x 1 wallpaper
White Brown Stripe       6 paint, carpet
                                      Wallpaper
                                     Combinations
 
There are 6 combinations of paint and carpet with striped wallpaper and 6
combinations of paint and carpet with solid wallpaper or 6 + 6 = 12
combinations.. That is p paint/carpet combinations x 2 wallpaper patterns =
12  possible combinations.
